About Us

Nareturaap Youth Group is based in Isiolo County, Kenya. We are dedicated to restoring degraded drylands and empowering youth through community-led environmental conservation.

Our Work

We build check dams, gabions, semi-circular bunds, and engage in reseeding and land restoration efforts.
